What is FacialPalsyPath?

This is an online educational hub created as a resource for learning about a facial paralysis diagnosis. We hope that this resource will help you find information and move forward in your journey with a bit more comfort & confidence.

Where did the name come from?

The name FacialPalsyPath was chosen to reflect the journey that people living with facial paralysis (aka facial palsy) go through, emphasizing the path of learning and understanding that comes with this condition.


Who can use this resource?

Anyone can use this educational site. Whether you are a patient with a recent or previous diagnosis, a friend or family member learning more about facial paralysis or a healthcare provider looking for resources for your patients.


How do I use this resource?

This resource is built to be a self-guided learning experience. This means that you can read the sections that you find to be most important for you without having to navigate through the entire site. Each section has unique information related to facial paralysis that you can explore at your own pace.

Disclaimer

FacialPalsyPath is an education resource for self-learning only.

This page does not act as a guide for diagnosis or treatment and does not substitute professional medical advice. FacialPalsyPath was created to act as a hub of information, but each case of facial paralysis is unique and each treatment path depends on the individual person. Please reach out to a qualified health care provider if you are experiencing symptoms you believe may be related to facial paralysis.
