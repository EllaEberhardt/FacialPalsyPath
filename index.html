FacialPalsyPath is a hub for learning about facial paralysis. Explore the anatomy of the facial nerve, the different stages of facial paralysis and learn more about your diagnosis at your own pace.

Jump Into Learning

What is FacialPalsyPath?

This is an online educational hub created as a resource for learning about a facial paralysis diagnosis. We hope that this resource will help you find information and move forward in your journey with a bit more comfort & confidence.

Where did the name come from?

The name FacialPalsyPath was chosen to reflect the journey that people living with facial paralysis (aka facial palsy) go through, emphasizing the path of learning and understanding that comes with this condition.


Who can use this resource?

Anyone can use this educational site. Whether you are a patient with a recent or previous diagnosis, a friend or family member learning more about facial paralysis or a healthcare provider looking for resources for your patients.


How do I use this resource?

This resource is built to be a self-guided learning experience. This means that you can read the sections that are the most important for you without having to navigate through the entire site. Each section has unique information related to facial paralysis that you can explore at your own pace.

Your First Care Point

Facial paralysis is a difficult diagnosis and it is important to stay kind to yourself. Every step in treatment, recovery and results is different for each person. It is not vain or self-centered to mourn the changes to your face and to want your old face back. Many people have shared that they feel self-conscious, or want to isolate themselves after developing facial paralysis. It is very important to ask for support and help through this time.
